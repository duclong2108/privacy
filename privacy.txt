Welcome to the Privacy Policy of Mirror Words. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly.

## INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### Information You Provide to Us

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password, and other profile information when you register.
- **User Content**: Any data you create, upload, or share through our app.
- **Communications**: Chat content, messages, or feedback when you contact us.

- **Device Data**: Device type, operating system, device identifiers, language settings.
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the app, time and frequency of use.

- **With Your Consent**: In other cases when we have your explicit consent.
